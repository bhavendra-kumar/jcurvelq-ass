## 1. Agent Thoughts

Agent thoughts are shown subtly (muted/italic) to maintain transparency without overwhelming non-technical users.
If deeper visibility is needed, this can evolve into a toggleable debug view.

---

## 2. Parallel Tasks

Parallel tasks are grouped under a labeled section to clearly communicate concurrent execution instead of implying a linear flow.

---

## 3. Partial Outputs

Intermediate outputs are streamed and preserved to show progress and build trust in the system’s reasoning process.

---

## 4. Cancelled Tasks

Cancelled tasks are styled neutrally (not as errors) to reflect intentional system optimization rather than failure.

---

## 5. Dependencies

Task dependencies are handled implicitly through ordering to avoid visual complexity while keeping execution understandable.

---

## 6. Final Output

The final result is highlighted in a separate, dominant panel to ensure users can quickly access the key insight.

---

## 7. Failures & Retries

Failures are shown clearly but with emphasis on recovery (e.g., retry states) to reflect system resilience.

---

## 8. Real-Time Simulation

A timed event emitter simulates real-time execution, enabling progressive UI updates without requiring a backend.

---

## Summary

The design focuses on clarity, real-time feedback, and strong hierarchy to make complex AI workflows understandable for non-technical users.
